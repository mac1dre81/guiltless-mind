# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the DocEditor Android application.

## Information Collection and Use

DocEditor is an offline-first application designed to respect your privacy. All processing happens locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal documents on our servers.

### Device and App Data

*   **Files and Documents:** The app uses Android's Storage Access Framework so you can open, edit, and save documents you explicitly choose. These files are processed entirely on your device and are not uploaded or shared by DocEditor.
*   **Camera Data:** If you use the Document Scanner feature (powered by Google ML Kit), the app requires camera access. The image processing for document scanning and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is performed entirely on your device. The scanned images and derived text remain under your control.

## Third-Party Services

The app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Google Play Services:** Used for Android platform integrations required by some app features.
*   **Google Play Billing:** Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not process or store your payment details directly; this is handled securely by Google.
*   **Google AdMob:** The free tier of the app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, which may collect and use advertising identifiers for personalized ads, subject to your device settings.
*   **Google ML Kit:** Used locally on your device for document scanning and text extraction. ML Kit does not send any image data to Google.

These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

## Security

We value your trust in providing us with the ability to read and manage your documents. We employ industry-standard encryption locally on your device where applicable (such as using DataStore for preferences). The app relies on the security of the Android OS and its permission system to sandbox its data. However, remember that no method of electronic storage on a personal device is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
