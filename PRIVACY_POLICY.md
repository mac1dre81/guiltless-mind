# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 16, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the DocEditor Android application.

## Information Collection and Use

DocEditor is an offline-first application designed to respect your privacy. Document viewing, editing, scanning, and OCR processing happen locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal documents on our servers.

### Device and App Data

*   **Files and Documents:** The app uses Android's Storage Access Framework so you can open, edit, and save documents you explicitly choose. These files are processed entirely on your device and are not uploaded or shared by DocEditor.
*   **Recent Files Metadata:** To make recent files available across app restarts, DocEditor stores the document URI string and the last-opened timestamp locally in app preferences. This metadata stays on your device.
*   **Camera Data:** If you use the Document Scanner feature (powered by Google ML Kit), the app requires camera access. Image capture, document scanning, and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) are performed locally on your device. The scanned images and derived text remain under your control.
*   **Backups:** DocEditor disables Android cloud/device backup for app data so internal app files, OCR outputs, and diagnostics are not automatically included in Android backup transfers by the app.

## Third-Party Services

The app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Google Play Services:** Used for Android platform integrations required by some app features.
*   **Google Play Billing:** Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not process or store your payment details directly; this is handled securely by Google.
*   **Google AdMob:** DocEditor includes Google AdMob SDK support for free-tier monetization. In releases where ads are enabled, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, diagnostics, and other data according to Google's own policies and your device settings.
*   **Google ML Kit / Google Play Services Document Scanner:** Used for document scanning and on-device text recognition. The app is designed to keep scanned images and OCR results on your device.

These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

## Security

We value your trust in providing us with the ability to read and manage your documents. We employ industry-standard encryption locally on your device where applicable (such as using DataStore for preferences). The app relies on the security of the Android OS and its permission system to sandbox its data. However, remember that no method of electronic storage on a personal device is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
